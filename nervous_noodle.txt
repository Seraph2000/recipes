Nervous Noodles - serves 1 hungry nerd

1. Boil some water - enough to fill a pan
2. Pour boiling water into the pan
3. Add 1 sachet of noodles to the boiling water
4. Leave noodles for 5 minutes
5. Heat a little bit of oil with 1 teaspoon of marmite in a pan
6. Add noodles
7. Rapidly stir noodles into marmite over heat
8. ENJOY!
